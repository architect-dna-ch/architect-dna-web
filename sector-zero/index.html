HOW IT WORKS

Simple. Lethal.

01
MOVE
WASD or arrow keys on desktop. Left half of screen is a virtual joystick on mobile.
02
AIM + SHOOT
Mouse cursor aims, hold left click to fire. On mobile, touch the right half to aim and shoot continuously.
03
CLEAR THE ROOM
Kill every enemy. Doors glow red until cleared. Collect dropped coins. Then move on.
04
WALK THROUGH THE DOOR
Once clear, approach any glowing door. The next room slides in. No menu. No loading. Just go.
05
FIND THE BOSS
The boss room is farthest from your start. Three phases. Expanding bullet spirals. Good luck. 🙂
