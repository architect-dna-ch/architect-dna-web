About the App
What is LIA?
LIA is a secure document vault designed for legal professionals and individuals who need to store sensitive legal files. Documents are end-to-end encrypted using keys derived on your device — Architect-DNA Systems has no access to your files.
Common Questions
What happens if I lose my device?
Your encryption keys are tied to your authenticated account. As long as you can log back in, your documents remain accessible after reinstalling the app.
Can Architect-DNA Systems read my documents?
No. Documents are encrypted on-device before upload. We have no access to the encryption keys and cannot read your files.
What data does LIA collect?
Only your email address, used for authentication via Supabase. No analytics, no advertising, no tracking. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Which law governs this service?
LIA is operated by Architect-DNA Systems, based in Switzerland. The service is governed by Swiss law.
