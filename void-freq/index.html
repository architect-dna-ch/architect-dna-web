Sound Channels

Five environments. One focus.

Every channel is synthesized in real time using the Web Audio API — no downloads, no buffering.

Deep Focus
Brown noise layered with a 40 Hz gamma carrier. Reduces distraction, promotes sustained attention.
Brown noise · 40Hz gamma
Rain Room
Filtered white noise with synthesized rainfall and randomised drip events. Steady and calming.
Bandpass noise · Rain drops
Void
A deep harmonic drone stack — sub-bass oscillators with a slow frequency sweep. Meditative and immersive.
Harmonic stack · Slow sweep
Lo-Fi
A live Am7 chord progression at 72 BPM with a synthesized drum machine and vinyl crackle texture.
Am7 loop · 72 BPM · Crackle
Neurons
