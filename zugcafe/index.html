How it works

Say what you're open to. Someone joins.

Every post is visible for exactly 5 minutes, to people within 2km. Then it's gone.

☕
Coffee
Post you're grabbing a coffee and open to company. Someone nearby taps join, you meet up.
🃏
Card game
Open a live UNO room right in the app — up to 4 players, no extra app to install.
🌿
Just company
Sometimes you just don't want to sit alone. Post it — no agenda needed.
Also inside

Built for the moment, not the profile.

💬
Rooms
Every join opens a private chat or game room — shareable by link, nearby-filtered so old rooms fade out of view after 24h.
🚆
Open Seats
Post where you'll be later — "Café Marta, Bern HB, 3pm" — no RSVP needed, people just show up.
🔒
No account
Just a name. Nothing to sign up for, nothing to manage. Location is only used to show what's nearby.
Get it on your phone

It's a website. It installs like an app.

1
Open zugcafe.architect-dna.ch in Safari or Chrome.
2
Tap Share → Add to Home Screen (iOS) or the install icon in the address bar (Android/Desktop).
3
It opens full-screen, with its own icon — no App Store needed.
