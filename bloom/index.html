Features

A garden that lives with you

No grinding. No pay-to-win. Just a calm growing world that moves at real time.

🌱
Real-Time Crops
Plant seeds and come back when they're ready. Tomatoes in 2 hours, sunflowers overnight. No fake acceleration.
🧁
Recipes & Kitchen
Combine harvested crops to cook recipes. Strawberry jam, carrot soup, blueberry muffins — unlock new dishes as you grow.
🏪
Market Stall
Sell your produce and cooked goods. Set your own prices. Neighbors browse your stall and leave coins.
💝
Gift Your Neighbors
Leave gift boxes in friends' gardens. Share rare seeds, surplus crops, or cooked goods with a personal note.
🌍
Visit Anyone
Walk through your neighbors' gardens. See how they've designed their space. Leave a reaction. Build a community.
🎨
Your Own Space
Decorate with paths, fences, benches and flowers. Every garden looks different. Yours reflects you.
