Daily Rhythm

12 minutes.
Your whole life, cared for.

FITRA structures your day into three brief rituals — morning intention, midday pulse, evening close — totalling under 12 minutes.

06:30 — MORNING

🌅 Open the day

Body check-in (sleep quality, first water). Set today's intention. One dua. One person to connect with. ~4 min

13:00 — MIDDAY

⚡ Pulse check

Hydration tap. Goal progress nudge. One learning card. Budget glance if Monday. ~2 min

21:00 — EVENING

🌙 Close the loop

Three daily wins. Evening reflection (AI-guided). Gratitude for one person. Dhikr. Journal if you want. ~6 min

AI Integration

Two models.
One for speed.
One for depth.

Quick check-ins use Claude Haiku — instant, lightweight, on-device-style responses that don't break your rhythm. Deep evening reflections use Claude Sonnet — synthesising your week's data into insights that actually move the needle.

All AI calls are scoped to your local data. No cloud storage. No training on your content. GDPR + DSG compliant by architecture.

Design Principles

Built different.
Intentionally.

🔒

Local-first

All data lives on your device. IndexedDB. No backend, no leak surface, no subscription for basic privacy.

⏱️

Time-aware

Features appear when they're relevant. Morning layers in the morning. Evening layers at night. No noise.

🚫

Anti-engagement

No streaks designed to guilt you. No gamification that hijacks dopamine. FITRA closes when you're done.

🌙

Faith-aware

Prayer times, Dua library, Dhikr counter, Quranic reflection — woven in naturally, never forced.

🇨🇭

Swiss-grade

GDPR + DSG compliant. Sub-200ms load. Deployed on Vercel Edge. Precision over flash.

🔗

Ecosystem-connected

Deep links into LIA (vault), ANWALT.GPT (law), bimpb apps (sleep, breathe, goals). One shell, many roots.
