Gameplay

How it works

🎯
Auto-Attack
Your character targets the nearest enemy automatically. Focus on movement, positioning, and chaining kills for streak multipliers.
⚡
4 Abilities
DASH bursts forward dealing impact damage. NOVA blasts nearby enemies outward. STORM chains lightning across multiple foes.
🪙
Coin Bank
Coins you collect carry over between runs. Spend them in the mid-run shop for temporary boosts, or save for the meta-shop for permanent upgrades.
💀
Boss Waves
Every 5th wave spawns a hexagonal boss with orbiting orbs and spread-shot. At 50% HP it enrages — speed and fire rate nearly double.
🛒
Mid-Run Shop
Shop opens every 3rd wave. Buy HP repairs, damage boosts, shields, twin-shot, and more. Press B anytime to browse during a run.
🌌
Arena Events
Random mid-wave events shake things up — meteor showers, void pulses that push everything outward, and frenzy mode that doubles enemy speed.

Enemies

Know your enemies

Melee · Wave 1+
Grunt
Fast and aggressive. Rushes directly at you and deals contact damage.
Ranged · Wave 3+
Archer
Fires projectiles from a distance and retreats when you get close.
Tank · Wave 5+
Brute
Slow, square-shaped, high HP. Drops a coin on death.
Armoured · Wave 6+
Shielded
Absorbs the first few hits with a rotating shield. Break the shield first, then finish it.
Support · Wave 8+
Healer
Pulses green aura that restores HP to nearby enemies. Priority target — kill it first.
Boss · Wave 5, 10…
The Nexus
Hexagonal boss with orbiting shield orbs. Fires spiral, cross, and barrage patterns. Enrages at 50% HP.
