Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Built for the moment you meet someone and want to be remembered.

📡
NFC Tap
Hold your phone to any NFC-enabled device. Your profile opens instantly in their browser — no app needed.
🔳
QR Code
Every Konnekt profile has a QR code. Show it, print it, put it on your desk. Scan = contact shared.
🌐
Browser-Native
Your profile lives at a URL. Nothing to download, nothing to install. Works on every phone, everywhere.
✏️
Always Up to Date
Change your number, title, or links anytime. Your card updates everywhere instantly — no reprinting.
🔗
Link Everything
LinkedIn, Instagram, portfolio, email, phone. Stack all your links in one clean profile.
🔒
Private & Simple
No account. No tracking. No subscription. Open source approach — your data stays yours.
